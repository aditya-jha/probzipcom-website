About Us

Founded by four IIT Roorkee Graduates, ProbZip began with a mission "to empower local economies by a connected ecosystem". By building On Demand Delivery infra for hyperlocal markets, we aim to bring communities closer. One problem at a time!
ProbZip is a logistics service that enables local businesses deliver goods with our algorithmically optimized approach. Through Probzip application, businesses can deliver in just two taps! We believe the role of technology and data is immense in operations.
